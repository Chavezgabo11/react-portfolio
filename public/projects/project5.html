Ashoka NGO

BRAND DESCRIPTION:

Ashoka is a non-governmental organization that targets and attempts to resolve conflicts around the world. This is via a network of social innovators that produce and act on ideas to provide solutions to communities in need.

PROJECT TYPE:

Branding, UX Design & Web Development

PROJECT ROLE:

Web Designer & Developer

TECHNOLOGIES USED:

HTML, CSS (SASS) & JS (Vue)

LOGO BREAKDOWN:

The logo is a rising sun to appeal to the new tomorrow we wish to see by creating positive change with our legion of changemakers. It also connects with one of the meanings behind the name “Ashoka” which was inspired by the Sanskrit word which meant “the active absence of sorrow” which I feel is nicely represented by a rising sun or new dawn.

Another hidden meaning of our design is the placement of the sun arches designed to look like the wi-fi connection symbol as a representation of how we connect our communities through change.

SOCIAL MEDIA:

We have our 3 preferred platforms here and each of them serves its own unique purpose:

Twitter is our Announcement Hub - the Primary source for social movements and discussion of them, the site functionality has lots of potential for engagement in responses which is perfect for what we need out of an announcement hub.

Instagram is the Event Hub - This platform typically has posts displaying the outcome of events having a mix of image and video posts, as well as posts directing people how to participate. It is also the center for the contest winners and engagement.

Youtube is the Activities platform – We use Youtube to showcase the events and videos shared by contestants that we feel best represent the idea behind the changemaker initiative and showcase them for a wider audience to see.

INSTAGRAM POSTS:

The Instagram examples are simple and direct, showing viewers how and what they need to do in order to join the contest with vibrant colours that connect with our brand, and eye-catching imagery.



TWITTER POSTS

The designs of twitter post examples, we choose very simple imagery and branding, while not shying away from more text filled designs to inform and direct viewers how they can join and what we are trying to present. We have also taken Joe’s feedback with his interest in the circle/line shape design and have experimented with creating designs using that motif.


YOUTUBE VIDEO CAMPAIGN:

The video campaign was developed to incite and promote changemakers challenge through visual advertisement, engaging our target audience.

With the video the goal is to highlight examples we feel are worthy to be displayed on a bigger stage. We have an example of a promotional video here today to show you how we can reach new viewers with different mediums.


ENVIRONMENT APPLICATION:

The environmental application is how our branding would appear in the real world.


UX & UI DESIGN:

The UX & UI design was developed through a creative process starting with a mockup, followed by a low fidelity wireframe with more details of how content will be distributed, and finally a high fidelity wireframe that shows the final design with its content.

MOBILE LAYOUT

The mobile layout adapts to small screens providing a better experience to mobile users.


WEB LAYOUT

The web layout adapts to large screens providing a better experience to laptops, desktops and tv users.
