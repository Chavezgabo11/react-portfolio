Glassware

BRAND DESCRIPTION:

Glassware brand is a glassware store that sells products in Canada made by artisans that represent our Canadian culture, creating national pride in the country, and making easy-to-sell products made by artisans throughout Canada.

PROJECT TYPE:

Branding, UX Design & Web Development

PROJECT ROLE:

Web Developer & Designer

TECHNOLOGIES USED:

HTML & CSS

LOGO BREAKDOWN:

The text component of the logo is “GLASSWARE” also including two glasses that represent the products that we sell in the store.

We also developed three logos each one will be used depending on the space or needs of the designs, which is also used to create a responsive design.


UX & UI DESIGN:

The UX & UI design was developed through a creative process starting with a mockup, followed by a low fidelity wireframe with more details of how content will be distributed, and finally a high fidelity wireframe that shows the final design with its content.

WEB LAYOUT

The web layout adapts to large screens providing a better experience to laptops, desktops and tv users.
